About

Talent Trail is a web-based career exploration tool that helps users discover careers that match their current skills. The application compares a user’s abilities with industry requirements and generates a compatibility report.

How to Use

  1. Search for a career you are interested in.
  2. Take the skills quiz for that career.
  3. View your compatibility score and skill gaps.
  4. Review recommended skills to improve.

About Page

Talent Trail is a web-based career gap analysis tool that generates personalized reports to help users pursue their professional goals. Users select a desired career and complete a survey detailing their current skills. Talent Trail then produces a report that analyzes skill gaps and outlines steps toward achieving that career.

Many people struggle to accurately evaluate their abilities and create a clear path toward their ideal career. The process can be overwhelming and time-consuming, often involving conflicting information from various sources.

Talent Trail simplifies this process by aligning real-world career requirements from the O*NET database with a user-filled survey, delivering a clear and actionable analysis.

Vision Statement

Talent Trail empowers users to explore careers by comparing their current qualifications with industry expectations. Using occupational data from the O*NET database, the platform generates a personalized gap analysis report that highlights match percentages and identifies areas for growth.
